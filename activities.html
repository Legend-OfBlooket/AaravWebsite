Activities

We have many fun activities waiting here just for you! A common day activity itinerary would include a breakfast meal at the Aravium Cafe for starting off the day. Then you could take a dive in the Rohunium Pool. After you have had a bath there, you can go playing around in the Ishnanium Party Centre!
